Hi, I'm Derek Nutile.

I've been around information systems since the early 1990's and consider myself a pretty well rounded technologist with a strength for efficiency and automation and a passion for making web applications.

This site illustrates what I consider to be a generic way to operate under perfect conditions. But a lot of what I purport to be best practices are hard or impossible to attain in every environment. The exercise here is simple: document what works, change it when it doesn't, and try to apply it where you can.
